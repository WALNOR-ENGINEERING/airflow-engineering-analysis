# Flow Behavior in Pipe Bend

## Engineering problem

Pipe bends often generate flow separation and secondary
vortices. These effects increase pressure losses and
may reduce system efficiency.

## Analysis

The airflow inside a 90° pipe bend was investigated to
identify vortex structures and regions of flow
separation.

## Key observations

• flow separation near the inner wall  
• vortex formation inside the bend  
• pressure losses across the bend  

## Engineering relevance

Understanding airflow behavior in pipe bends helps
improve duct design and reduce energy losses in
ventilation systems.
