# Cyclone Separator Airflow Analysis

## Engineering problem

Cyclone separators are widely used in industrial
filtration systems to remove dust particles from
airflow.

Separation efficiency strongly depends on the internal
swirling flow structure.

## Analysis

The airflow inside a cyclone separator was studied to
observe vortex formation and pressure distribution.

## Key observations

• strong swirling airflow inside the cyclone  
• recirculation zones near the outlet  
• pressure drop across the separator  

## Engineering relevance

Understanding airflow inside cyclone separators allows
engineers to improve separation efficiency and reduce
energy losses.
