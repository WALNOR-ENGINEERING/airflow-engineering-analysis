# Engineering problem

Efficient ventilation systems require
stable and uniform airflow distribution.

Poor airflow behavior may create:

• dead airflow zones  
• poor air exchange  
• uneven ventilation  
• thermal discomfort  

---

# Analysis overview

Airflow distribution inside a ventilated space
was analyzed to observe
air movement and recirculation behavior.

---

# Key observations

• airflow jet development  
• recirculation zones  
• uneven airflow distribution  
• low velocity regions  

---

# Engineering relevance

Airflow analysis helps optimize:

• industrial ventilation  
• HVAC systems  
• airflow distribution  
• air exchange efficiency
