# Pipe Flow Pressure Loss Analysis

![Cover](cover.png)

---

# Engineering problem

Pressure losses inside piping systems directly influence:

• fan performance  
• airflow stability  
• energy consumption  
• operational efficiency  

Understanding airflow behavior inside pipes
is essential for efficient industrial airflow systems.

---

# Analysis overview

The airflow inside a circular pipe was analyzed
to observe turbulence behavior,
velocity distribution and pressure losses.

The study focuses on flow development
along the pipe and the influence
of turbulence on system efficiency.

---

# Key observations

• turbulence development along the pipe  
• pressure loss behavior  
• velocity profile formation  
• wall friction influence  

---

# Engineering relevance

Understanding pressure losses helps improve:

• piping system efficiency  
• airflow stability  
• energy consumption  
• industrial ventilation performance
