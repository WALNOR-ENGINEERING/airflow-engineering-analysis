# Industrial Airflow Optimization

![Cover](cover.png)

---

# Engineering problem

Industrial airflow systems often contain:

• unstable flow regions  
• excessive turbulence  
• unnecessary pressure losses  
• uneven airflow distribution  

These effects reduce system efficiency
and increase operational costs.

---

# Analysis overview

The airflow system was analyzed
to identify problematic regions
and evaluate optimization possibilities.

---

# Key observations

• unstable airflow regions  
• pressure loss concentration  
• turbulence accumulation  
• inefficient airflow transitions  

---

# Proposed improvements

• smoother airflow transitions  
• geometry optimization  
• reduced turbulence regions  
• improved airflow distribution  

---

# Engineering relevance

Flow optimization helps reduce:

• operational costs  
• energy consumption  
• airflow instability  
• system inefficiency
