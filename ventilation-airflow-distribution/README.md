# Ventilation Airflow Distribution

## Engineering problem

Proper airflow distribution is essential in ventilation
systems to ensure effective air exchange and thermal
comfort.

## Analysis

Airflow patterns inside a simplified room ventilation
system were analyzed to observe how air spreads
throughout the space.

## Key observations

• airflow jet development from inlet  
• recirculation regions inside the room  
• air distribution patterns  

## Engineering relevance

Airflow analysis helps engineers design ventilation
systems that provide uniform air distribution and
efficient air exchange.
